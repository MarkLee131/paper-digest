## Writing & Rationale Insights

A zero-volume day is itself a small data point about Scholar's alert cadence — recommendation digests are not strictly daily, and a quiet day is the expected long-tail behaviour rather than a pipeline failure. Logging the zero-day explicitly (rather than silently skipping) keeps the report index honest and makes it easier to spot a *real* outage if the inbox stays empty for several days in a row.
